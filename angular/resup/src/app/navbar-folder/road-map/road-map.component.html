Welcome to Rustume

Named "Rustume," this platform ingeniously merges the concepts of "rust" and "resume" to serve as a comprehensive showcase of my expertise and achievements. It functions as a centralized repository, meticulously detailing my educational pursuits, technical acumen, and professional accomplishments, while also offering seamless access for past collaborators seeking references.

Software

The development of the Rustume website was meticulously executed on the Ubuntu Linux platform, harnessing the power of the Rust programming language in conjunction with the Rocket framework for backend operations. The decision to adopt Rust stemmed from its unparalleled support for concurrency, remarkable speed, and granular control, all of which are pivotal for establishing a learning-centric environment. Additionally, Rust's modern syntax, coupled with its stringent compiler, inherently promotes best coding practices, thus elevating the overall quality of the codebase. Rust Rocket emerged as the natural choice for the backend framework, owing to its adaptability and ability to afford precise control over the website's functionalities, thereby facilitating a deeper comprehension of web development paradigms. Complementing this, Angular was seamlessly integrated for frontend development, offering a streamlined approach to project maintenance and providing extensive support for a myriad of libraries aimed at enhancing frontend functionalities. Furthermore, AWS was strategically chosen as the hosting solution, ensuring unwavering performance and scalability. This judicious selection of technologies underscores a deliberate endeavor to construct an efficient and sustainable web platform, while concurrently fostering a culture of continuous learning and exploration within the development realm.

Learning Journey

Notably, the deployment architecture involves AWS load balancers distributed across six subnets within the us-east-1a and us-east-1b regions, each dedicated to program execution, database management, and bastion host operation. Leveraging tools such as Route 53, RDS, ECS, and ECR further enhances the platform's resilience and scalability.

Website Overview

The development journey of Rustume has been marked by an enriching learning experience, encompassing mastery of various cutting-edge technologies. This includes proficiency in Docker, encompassing the creation and deployment of Docker images, alongside a thorough understanding of Rust Rocket, which delved into diverse networking considerations, security features, and an in-depth exploration of HTTP protocol intricacies. Moreover, delving into the AWS ecosystem necessitated a comprehensive understanding of its various features, including RDS, ECS, load balancing, ECR, and the intricacies of subnet configuration.
